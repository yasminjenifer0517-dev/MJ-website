STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

Ride Stopped Midway

An interactive guide for Captains & Support Executives navigating unexpected trip interruptions safely and professionally.

SCENARIO 01

Vehicle Breakdown

SITUATION

The captain's bike breaks down during the trip due to a mechanical issue (engine failure, chain snap, etc.), making it impossible to continue.

Required Actions (SOP)

01

Inform immediately

Inform the customer immediately about the breakdown in a calm, clear tone.

02

Apologize

Apologize sincerely for the unexpected delay and inconvenience caused to their schedule.

03

Safely stop

Pull over and park the vehicle in a safe, designated spot off the main roadway traffic.

04

Arrange alternative

Help the customer book an alternative Rapido ride on their phone or assist them in contacting customer support.

05

Report issue

Ensure the trip issue is logged and marked as a breakdown directly in the Captain app dashboard.

SCENARIO 02

Flat Tyre / Puncture

SITUATION

The Captain's bike gets a puncture midway through the journey, compromising the safety and control of the vehicle.

Required Actions (SOP)

01

Stop Safely

Safely decelerate and bring the vehicle to a halt in a secure location, avoiding sudden braking.

02

Explain Clearly

Explain the flat tyre situation clearly to the customer, displaying the physical issue to establish trust.

03

Alternative Ride

Assist the customer in booking an alternative ride or arranging alternative transport, preventing them from being stranded.

04

Safe Spot Assurance

Verify that the customer is in a safe, secure public area before the Captain leaves to fix the tyre.

SCENARIO 03

Medical Emergency

SITUATION

The Captain suddenly feels unwell, dizzy, or suffers an injury/medical episode during the ride, making it unsafe to ride.

Required Actions (SOP)

01

Stop in Public Area

Immediately pull over in a safe, well-lit public area. Turn off the engine.

02

Inform the Customer

Inform the customer politely about feeling unwell. Do not force yourself to continue driving.

03

Contact Support

Contact emergency services (if needed) or the Rapido Support Team immediately for assistance.

04

Arrange Another Ride

Assist the customer in getting another ride so they can reach their destination safely.

SCENARIO 04

Route Blocked / Heavy Traffic

SITUATION

The planned route is blocked by an accident, waterlogging/flooding, police barricades, or intense traffic congestion that prevents progress.

Required Actions (SOP)

01

Inform Customer

Communicate the roadblock/traffic issue clearly to the customer, explaining why you have stopped.

02

Suggest Alternate Route

Propose an alternate path if available, and check if the customer is okay with navigating the redirection.

03

Help Arrange Alternative

If the ride cannot continue or alternate routes are impossible, cancel the ride and assist the customer in securing a different form of travel.

04

Report in App

Log the cancellation in the app under "Route Blocked" to ensure support logs the traffic/route discrepancy.

SCENARIO 05

Safety Concern

SITUATION

The Captain spots extremely hazardous road conditions (e.g. violent protests, sudden severe weather, localized unrest, or dangerous drivers).

Required Actions (SOP)

01

Prioritize Safety

Safety is the absolute priority. Do not attempt to force your way through a dangerous area.

02

Stop in Secure Location

Pull over and secure the vehicle at the nearest safe spot (e.g. public hotel, gas station, metro station).

03

Explain the Reason

Calmly explain the safety risk to the customer and assure them of their personal safety.

04

Contact Support

Call support immediately to report the danger zone so other Captains can be rerouted/warned.

SCENARIO 06

Customer Requests Midway End

SITUATION

The customer requests to end the ride early due to personal reasons, meeting changes, or wanting to walk the rest of the way.

Required Actions (SOP)

01

Confirm Request

Politely confirm the customer's request, asking if everything was satisfactory during the trip.

02

End Ride correctly

End the ride immediately in the app at the current location to ensure proper GPS and fair calculation.

03

Ensure Safe Drop-off

Ensure the customer is dropped off in a safe location, especially at night or on busy expressways.

ALWAYS APPLICABLE

Key SOP Rules Checklist

Mandatory rules that every Captain and Executive must execute in any ride-stopped-midway event.

Customer Safety First

Under all circumstances, the physical security and well-being of the customer is our primary directive.

Clear Communication

Explain the reason for stopping transparently and professionally without causing panic.

No Extra Cash Demands

Never request cash or adjustments outside the app. The system automatically computes fare breakdowns.

Log in Support Channels

Report the interruption via the official app support panel or contact supervisor hotlines immediately.

Polite & Professional

Remain calm and courteous throughout, keeping in mind that the customer is experiencing a disruption.
